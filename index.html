That makes sense. When someone we love is gone, it’s normal to feel like there’s a hole in our life. Would you like to share a memory of him?

That sounds like such a special memory. Holding onto those moments can help you feel connected to him. It’s okay to miss him and still feel grateful for those times.

I just wish I had more time with him.

I know. It’s hard when there’s so much left unsaid. But it’s okay to grieve in your own time. You don’t have to go through this alone.

Common Challenges in Grief

Feeling Alone

It’s easy to feel isolated when you’re grieving. Talking to someone—even a chatbot—can help you feel less alone.

Processing Unspoken Feelings

Sometimes it’s hard to put emotions into words. Everlight gently helps you explore and express those hidden feelings.

Coping with Triggers

Memories, places, and even certain dates can stir up emotions. Everlight provides strategies to help you manage these moments.

How It Works

Step 1: Start the conversation by sharing how you feel.

Step 2: The chatbot will respond with empathy and helpful guidance.

Step 3: Receive personalized support and resources to help you cope.

Step 4: Reflect and revisit the conversation anytime you need comfort.

Benefits of Talking About Grief

  • Sharing your emotions helps reduce feelings of loneliness and isolation.
  • Expressing grief can improve emotional regulation and mental clarity.
  • Talking about your loss helps honor your loved one's memory and process emotions.
  • Receiving supportive feedback can help you find strength and resilience.
